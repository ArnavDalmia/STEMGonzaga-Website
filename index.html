Should I join?

Are you someone who is intrested in the field of Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathamatics. Do you like to solve puzzles, brain teasers and riddles. Then STEM is the right club for you.Our club is designed to help students who are intrested in going into a STEM field by exposing them to oppurtunities to expand their knowledge and intrest about their field. Some of these oppurtunities include lectures and Q and A sessions from real life computer scientists, engineers and so many more professionals. We also dedicate weeks to Question and Asnwer weeks specifically geared towards answering the many question students have about university and college applications. We offer activities and opporunities exploring the different aspects of STEM to help our members understand what they like.
