Should I join?

Are you someone who is interested in Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics? Do you like to solve puzzles, brainteasers and riddles? Then STEM is the right club for you.Our club helps students who are interested in going into a STEM field by exposing them to opportunities to expand their knowledge and interest in their field. Some of these opportunities include lectures and Q&A sessions from real life computer scientists, engineers and so many more professionals. We also dedicate weeks to Question and Answer weeks specifically geared towards answering the many questions students have about university and college applications. We offer activities and opportunities exploring the different aspects of STEM to help our members understand what they like.
