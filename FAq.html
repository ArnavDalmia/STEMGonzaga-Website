Frequently Asked Questions



What is STEM?

STEM is an acronym for Science Technology Engineering and Mathematics. Our club
is designed to help students ace their STEM classes and introduce them to a career in the field of STEM

What would a typical session look like?

A typical session would be either a fun STEM related activity, a Q and A session with someone who has
gone through the STEM career path as well as recordings with professionals or grad students currently in the STEM profession

When do we have meetings?

Sessions are held every 2 weeks. The session date and time is usually at the top of this website.
If it is not displayed here do not hesitate to contact our executives (Socials found below)

When can I join?

You can join at any time!!! The great thing about stem is that it is not a team,2e are not strict on attendance.
However, attending all the meetings will benefit you and will help you grow as a STEM warrior :)

Will there be any competitions?

For now we are working towards the Science Olympics. But in the future we are planning for more but not confirmed just yet. Stay Tuned!
